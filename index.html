Cherry Blossom Tree Facts

Interesting facts~

  • Cherry blossom trees are native to Japan, but are now grown in many countries around the world.
  • Cherry blossom trees typically bloom in early spring, lasting for about two weeks.
  • There are over 200 varieties of cherry blossom trees.
  • Cherry blossom trees are also called Sakura trees in Japan.
  • Cherry blossom festivals are held in many countries around the world, including the United States.
